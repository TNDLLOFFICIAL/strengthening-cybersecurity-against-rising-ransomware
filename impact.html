Attacks on Critical National Infrastructure (CNI) represent the highest risk tier. When hospital records or energy grids are compromised, the impact shifts from financial loss to a direct threat to human life and national security.

The Ethical Paradox

Organizations face an ethical crisis: paying a ransom funds future criminal research and development, yet refusing to pay can lead to permanent data loss for innocent clients. The 2026 legal framework is moving toward making ransom payments globally illegal to break this cycle.

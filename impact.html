Societal Impact

Analyzing how digital vulnerability reshapes the physical world.

01. The Physicality of Cyber Warfare

For decades, cyber attacks were seen as virtual nuisances. Today, the line between the digital and physical worlds has evaporated. We now live in an era where lines of code can shut down power grids and paralyze hospital operations.

"Cybersecurity is no longer about protecting data; it's about protecting lives."

Critical Infrastructure Vulnerability

Most of the world's critical infrastructure runs on SCADA systems. When these systems are connected to the web, they become targets for attacks designed to cause physical destruction.

02. Economic Devaluation

  • Intellectual Property Theft: State-sponsored actors steal trade secrets, allowing foreign competitors to leapfrog decades of innovation.
  • Trust Erosion: Once a customer's personal data is leaked, the brand damage is often permanent.

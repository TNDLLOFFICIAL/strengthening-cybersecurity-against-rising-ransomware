Why This Project Matters

Saving Small Businesses

Over 60% of small businesses go out of business within 6 months of a cyber attack because they can't afford the legal fees and the lost data. SEC-OPS provides the basic knowledge that these businesses need to stay alive. We aren't just protecting computers; we're protecting people's livelihoods.

Empowering Students

Every student should know how to defend their own data. By making these topics easy to understand, we are giving our peers the power to use the internet without fear. Knowledge is the ultimate firewall, and we are distributing that knowledge for free.

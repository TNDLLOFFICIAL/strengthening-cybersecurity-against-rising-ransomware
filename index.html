Digital Safety for Everyone.

We take the scary, complicated parts of the internet and explain them in a way that makes sense. Learn how to protect your passwords, your photos, and your privacy.

Why SEC-OPS?

Most websites about hacking use words that are too hard to understand. SEC-OPS was built for students and regular people. We believe that if you understand how a lock works, you'll be much better at keeping your own "Digital House" safe.

What You'll Find Here

On this site, we show you the most common ways hackers try to trick you, the science of how secret codes keep your data private, and simple solutions you can use today to make your phone and computer un-hackable.

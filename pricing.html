Common Queries

Detailed insights into our billing and implementation cycles.

Can I upgrade my tier during an active attack?

Yes. Our system allows for "Hot Swapping" protection tiers. If your Sentry node detects volumetric traffic exceeding its capacity, you can instantly scale to Core Mesh without dropping existing legitimate connections.

What happens if I exceed my traffic limit?

We do not hard-cap security. In the event of a traffic surge, SEC-OPS continues protection at a metered rate of $0.05 per GB, ensuring your infrastructure stays online during critical growth periods.

Is there a setup fee for Sovereign deployments?

Sovereign Tiers involve a one-time architecture audit and kernel mapping fee, which varies based on the complexity of your existing network topology.

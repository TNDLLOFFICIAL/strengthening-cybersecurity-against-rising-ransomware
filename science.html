The Science of Secret Codes

What is Cryptography?

It’s the way computers lock up your information. When you type a password, the computer turns it into a secret code that looks like random gibberish. This way, even if a hacker steals the file, they can't read what's inside without the "Key."

[Image of symmetric vs asymmetric encryption diagram]

Why do we use it?

Every time you buy something online or send a private text, you are using cryptography. It is the only thing keeping the internet from being a place where everyone can see everyone else's private business.

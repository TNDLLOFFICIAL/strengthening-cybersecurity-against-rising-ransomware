The Science of Secret Codes

Cryptography: The Digital Lock

What is it? Cryptography is just a fancy word for "Secret Codes." Imagine you want to send a note to your friend, but you don't want anyone else to read it. You scramble the letters so they look like nonsense. Your friend is the only one who knows the "Key" to unscramble them.

Why does it matter? Every time you buy something on Amazon or log into TikTok, your password and credit card info are turned into these secret codes. Even if a hacker steals the data, they can't read it because it's scrambled.

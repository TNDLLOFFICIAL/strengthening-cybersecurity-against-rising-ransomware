The Science: Cryptography

Secret Codes (Encryption)

What is it? Imagine you have a message that says "HELLO." Before you send it, your computer scrambles it into "X8#qL." This scrambled mess is what travels across the internet. If a hacker intercepts it, they have no idea what it means.

The Key: The person you sent the message to has a special "Digital Key" that unscrambles "X8#qL" back into "HELLO." This whole process is called Cryptography. It is the science that keeps your credit card numbers and private texts safe every single day.

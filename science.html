The Science: Cryptography

How we use "Secret Codes" to protect your messages.

1. What is Cryptography?

Think of it like a Digital Vault. If you send a text, you don't want a hacker to read it. Cryptography turns your "Hello" into a bunch of random gibberish like "xY9!pQ". Only the person you sent it to has the "Key" to turn it back into "Hello."

2. Why does this matter to the Sharks?

Every bank, app, and government in the world uses this. Without these secret codes, your credit card numbers would be visible to everyone on the internet. We teach the math that keeps the world's money safe.

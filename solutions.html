Our Defense Solutions

Hacking is a big problem, but these tools make you nearly un-hackable.

Solution #1

The Firewall Guard

A firewall is your first line of defense. Think of it as a security guard for your home Wi-Fi. It checks every piece of data trying to come in or leave your network. If the data looks like a virus or comes from a known hacker's computer, the firewall slams the door and blocks it immediately.

Without this, your computer would be constantly bombarded by automated bots trying to find a way into your files.

Solution #2

Multi-Factor Auth (MFA)

Passwords are no longer enough. MFA is like having two different locks on your front door. Even if a hacker steals your password, they still need your physical phone to get the special login code. This one simple step stops 99% of bulk hacking attempts instantly.

Solution #3

Secure Protocols (HTTPS)

Always look for the little padlock in your browser's address bar. That padlock means the website is using HTTPS. This is a set of rules that forces the website to encrypt everything you type. If you are on a site that only uses "HTTP" (no S), hackers on the same Wi-Fi as you can see your password as you type it.

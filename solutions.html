How We Solve It

Firewalls

A firewall is like a digital wall around your computer. It checks every piece of information trying to get in. If it looks suspicious or comes from a known "bad" source, the firewall blocks it immediately.

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

This is the best way to stay safe. Even if a hacker steals your password, they can't get in because the app will send a special code to your phone. Unless they have your physical phone, they are stuck outside.

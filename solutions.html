How to Stay Safe

1. Firewalls

A firewall is like a digital security guard for your network. It checks every piece of information trying to enter your computer. If the information comes from a dangerous place or looks like a virus, the firewall blocks it before it can do any damage.

2. 2FA (Two-Factor)

This is the best tool we have. Even if a hacker steals your password, they can't get into your account because the app sends a special code to your phone. Unless they have your physical phone in their hand, they are stuck outside.

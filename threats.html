Common Digital Threats

Phishing (The Trick)

This is when a hacker sends you an email that looks exactly like a real one from Netflix or Instagram. They tell you to "Log in to fix an error," but when you type your password, they steal it. Always check the email address before clicking!

Malware (The Spy)

Malware is bad software that hides in "free" downloads or game mods. Once it's on your computer, it can watch your screen, steal your saved passwords, or even use your webcam without you knowing.

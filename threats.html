Understanding Digital Threats

Social Engineering (The Human Hack)

This is the most common way people get hacked. Instead of using fancy code, hackers use Psychology. They send emails or DMs pretending to be a friend, a teacher, or a company like Instagram. They create a "fake emergency" to trick you into clicking a link and typing your password. Remember: If an email sounds scary and asks you to log in immediately, it's probably a trap.

Ransomware (The Digital Kidnapping)

Ransomware is a type of virus that locks all the files on your computer. You can't see your photos or schoolwork anymore. The hackers then display a message saying you have to pay them hundreds of dollars in Bitcoin to get your files back. This happens most often when people download "cracked" games or suspicious attachments.

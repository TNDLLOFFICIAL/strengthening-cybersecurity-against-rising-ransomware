Digital Threats

Phishing

Think of this like a "fake mailman." They send emails that look like they're from your bank or Discord, but they're just trying to steal your login keys.

Malware & Ransomware

Software designed to "kidnap" your files. Ransomware locks your computer and demands money to get your homework or photos back.

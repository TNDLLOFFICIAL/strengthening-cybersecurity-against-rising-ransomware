Common Digital Threats

Phishing (The Trick)

Phishing is when a hacker sends you a fake message that looks real. For example, you might get an email that looks exactly like it's from Instagram saying "Someone tried to log in, click here to change your password." When you click the link and type your password, the hacker steals it instantly. They use trickery instead of "hacking" to get what they want.

Malware (The Virus)

Malware stands for "Malicious Software." This is a bad app or file that gets onto your computer, usually when you download something "free" like a game mod or a movie. Once it's in, it can record your screen, steal your saved bank info, or even use your microphone to listen to you.

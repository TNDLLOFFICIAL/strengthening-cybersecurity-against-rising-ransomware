Understanding the landscape of modern digital threats is the first step toward building resilient infrastructure. SEC-OPS monitors emerging risk factors and identifies patterns of unauthorized network behavior in real-time.

Primary Risk Classifications

We categorize infrastructure risks based on their potential impact on data integrity and system availability.

Critical Severity

Volumetric Traffic Saturation

Attempts to overwhelm network gateways with high-frequency packet requests, aimed at degrading performance and causing widespread service denial for legitimate users.

MITIGATION: Adaptive Rate Limiting
Critical Severity

Cryptographic Interception

Exploiting vulnerabilities in transit protocols to capture and decrypt data streams. This targeting focuses on legacy algorithms and unverified handshake sequences.

MITIGATION: Forced RSA-4096 Standards
Elevated Awareness

Identity Spoofing

Malicious attempts to replicate device fingerprints or user credentials to gain unauthorized node access.

Elevated Awareness

Lateral Escalation

The process by which an unauthorized user attempts to move from a peripheral node to the core infrastructure.

Elevated Awareness

Logic Injections

Inserting unauthorized instructions into application layers to manipulate or extract system-level data.

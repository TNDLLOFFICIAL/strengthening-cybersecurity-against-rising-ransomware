Digital Threats

To stay safe, you have to know how hackers attack. Here are the most common ways people get hacked today.

1. Phishing (Fake Messages)

This is when you get an email or text that looks like it's from a real company (like Netflix or Roblox), but it’s actually a trap. They want you to click a link and type your password so they can steal your account.

2. Malware (Hidden Viruses)

Malware is "Bad Software" that gets onto your phone or computer. It can sit in the background and record what you type, or it can lock all your files and demand you pay money to get them back.

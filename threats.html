Threat Intelligence

The Topic: Digital Scouting
Intelligence means knowing what the bad guys are doing before they do it. We track hackers so you don't get caught by surprise.

Phishing Scams

This is when a hacker sends you a fake email (like from "Instagram") asking you to log in. They aren't actually Instagram—they are just trying to steal your password.

Malware (Viruses)

These are "Bad Apps" that spy on your computer. They can record your screen or even lock your files until you pay the hacker money.
